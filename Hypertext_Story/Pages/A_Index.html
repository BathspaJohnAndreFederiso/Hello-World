THE STORY

You might have heard of this one before...

In this story, you play as a big, bad wolf. Your neighbors, the three little pigs, don't interact with you as much. On one very important morning (your Grandma's birthday), you find yourself short of some sugar for a cake. Maybe your neighbors might have some...

HOW TO PLAY

  • To navigate throughout the page, simply click on the displayed choices. These choices are found at the bottom of the page. Pay close attention to the text and choose wisely, as you cannot return to the previous page...
  • You can start by clicking on the link at the bottom of this page.
  • You can go back to the start of the story at any point by clicking on the HOME icon at the top of the page.
  • Have fun!
