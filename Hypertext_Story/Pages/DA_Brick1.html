You decide to visit your pig neighbor that lives in the brick house. It's the farthest house out of the three brothers so it will take you a lot of time, time that you can't afford to waste. After some time spent walking you spot your objective, the brick abode near a hill and a small road.

Once you arrive at the house, you knock on the door.

Nobody answered. You knock again. This time, you hear the pattering of someone's footsteps behind the door. The door's peep slot opens abruptly, and you see your neighbor giving you the evil eye.

"What do you want?"

You hear him bellow from behind the door. How rude, you haven't even said anything yet! You ask him for some sugar.
