You decide to visit your pig neighbor that lives in the stick house. It's a bit of a long walk from there, but you make it just before noon. You soon arrive at a very shoddy shack, made out of sticks. It's a wonder that your neighbor actually lives in something that can be easily toppled. You knock as gently as you can on the house's entrance.

"O-open that door and you'll really regret it!" You hear the house's occupant scream out, followed by what seems to be the sound of a gun cocking! You instinctively step aside, as if he's going to shoot through the door right then and there. What's his problem, anyway?

You, very politely, ask him if he has what you need.

...the door creaks very slowly. A long, metal barrel peeks out between the door and the frame, then followed by the rest of the rifle, held by a hairy, pink pig man. He looks like he hasn't slept for days.

“ Come any closer and I won't hesitate! If what you're saying is true...then what do you want?!"
