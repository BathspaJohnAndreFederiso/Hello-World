He tells you that there’s something attacking his property these past few weeks, and recently they’ve been getting more violent. Giving the surroundings a look-over, his claims hold some water. Along the wall there are multiple scratches in many lengths and sizes. Some parts of the house look very weak, as if a heavy wind blew over the house, with one part of the roof looking like it's been hastily repaired recently. The fence is missing a few pieces too.

The most notable piece of evidence however, is the set of fresh, wolfish footprints. Examining the path of the tracks, it looks as if the culprit came from the forest, to the house and then went back the way they came.
