You hang around for a bit, seeing if you had missed anything of importance.
And in fact, you did. You find that there is a trail of footprints leading back into the forest. Upon closer inspection they looked very canine, eerily similar to your own...
Suddenly, you hear some commotion come from the forest.

Eventually, the continued noises gave way to the sound of clamouring of people and voices. You watch as a group of people from the nearby town, led by a pig man (your other neighbor?) surround the area. Looks like they've been following the trail too.
"See! I t-told you it was one of them wolf-folks, up to no good!"
The pig man leading the group stutters, pointing at you. As if on cue, the mob surrounds you. You very quickly realize this isn't a good look for you, being on the site of what might be a crime with evidence painting YOU as the culprit.
