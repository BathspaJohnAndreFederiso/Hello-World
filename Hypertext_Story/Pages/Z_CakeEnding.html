CAKE ENDING

"I'm glad you asked! I actually have some right now that I can spare." He says in between mouthfuls of bread. You tell him that’s wonderful news. Both of you begin chatting away to pass the time. After you both finish your chat and snacks, he leaves for the kitchen, presumably to get the much-needed sugar. "That was easy." you thought.

With your quest complete and sugar in hand, you went home right away to get started. It took many hours of hard work, even longer to get all the fur out of it, but you made an excellent cake worthy of your grandmother!

The party was wonderful, but Grandma didn't feel like eating that day...

THE END
